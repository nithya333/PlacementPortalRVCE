Our algorithms match students with opportunities across various industries, ensuring you find the perfect fit for your career goals.

1. Registration and Profile Creation

The recruitment process begins with students registering on the Charitham platform. Upon registration, students create their profiles by filling in essential details such as academic qualifications, skills, interests, and career aspirations. A well-crafted profile enables recruiters to easily identify the most suitable candidates for their requirements.

2. Personalized Job Recommendations

Once the profile is complete, the platform provides personalized job recommendations based on the student's qualifications, skills, and interests. These recommendations are generated through advanced algorithms that match students with opportunities across various industries. Students can explore and apply for jobs that align with their career goals.

3. Application Process

After reviewing job opportunities, students can apply directly through the Charitham platform. The application process typically involves submitting a resume, cover letter, and any relevant documents such as certifications or portfolios. Some positions may also require students to take assessments or answer pre-interview questions as part of the application.

4. Interview and Assessment

If shortlisted, students are invited for interviews. The interview process may include a combination of technical and behavioral interviews, depending on the role. In some cases, recruiters may also conduct technical assessments or online tests to evaluate the student's skills. The Charitham platform ensures that students are prepared with resources such as interview tips, mock interviews, and industry insights.

5. Offer and Selection

Based on interview performance and assessment results, students who meet the criteria are selected for job offers. The offer typically includes details such as the job title, compensation package, job location, and other relevant terms. Charitham provides a seamless platform for students to review and accept job offers.
